SP Broadway is a public relations consultancy specialising in planning and development. They came to us as their site was not responsive and needed to be modernised.

Plan

We had meetings with the client to understand their business and conducted some competitor research to understand the planning and development landscape. With this information to hand we went about re-designing the website.

Design

The client had a gallery of images that they were not effectively using online. By utilising these images and reformatting them to be full width we were able to incorporate them into the layout and produce a fresh, modern site with simple and easy to use navigation. We also designed a brochure and newsletter in keeping with the new website design.

Develop

We implemented the site into a new installation of WordPress which enabled the site to be responsive. We created editable modules for the team, case studies, events and politics of planning as well as a newsletter sign up form.
