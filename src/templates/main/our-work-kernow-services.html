Kernow services is a local, reliable and highly skilled property, maintenance and building services company in Cornwall. With no online presence they contacted us to create their branding and website.

Plan

We worked closely with the client to understand the types of jobs they undertake in order to find the right imagery for the website.

Design

We started by creating a vibrant, modern visual representation for the firm. The logo had to work in an online and print format but also had to be suitable for the signage on Kernow's fleet of vehicles. Once the logo was approved, we created a colour palette and went on to select imagery that really highlighted the types of maintenance work that Kernow offers, whilst also conveying the company's strong Cornish routes.

Develop

The site was developed in a content management system called Drupal. This CMS enabled the client to upload information about projects they have undertaken and show them on the website. The site was also developed to be responsive so can be viewed on any device.
