www.pomarina.com

Pomarina is a premium sparkling cider made in Spain. The brief was to create a website for the UK market to promote and sell this product.

← Our work

Plan

We used Pomarina's brand guidelines to produce a website that set them apart from their competitors as an exclusive cider that tastes as good as it looks.

Design

The client wanted the design to really highlight the individuality of the product and to clearly communicate how Pomarina is not just any cider but a 'champagne' cider - like no other. It was also important for the design and layout to convey the brand's rich history and place of origin. Each page has a bespoke design which helps to bring the brand to life.

Develop

We developed this site in Typo3 which allows the client to update content and the news section on a regular basis. The site was built responsively for all devices and is intuitive for users to navigate.
