Smart Money is a specialist finance broker. Their brief was to re-design their site to make it more modern and professional. They also wanted to update some of their functionality in order for it to be more intuitive.

Plan

Having been let down by another agency it was important to spend some time with the client and really understand what they liked and disliked about their current site and also other sites.

Design

We took the current content and re-designed and configured the layout to produce a clean, sophisticated site that exudes professionalism. The large rotating banners clearly portray the 4 services that Smart Money offers and the rest of the site content is now easily accessible and intuitive to find.

Develop

The site was already in WordPress but we updated the version and code and made the site responsive so that it can be viewed on any device. We also redesigned and re-developed one of the forms to be more user-friendly and created another form.
