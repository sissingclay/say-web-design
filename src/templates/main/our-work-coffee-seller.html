Coffee Seller is an independent coffee wholesaler. The company had a functioning website but they weren’t happy with the design so we went about re-designing the website for them.

Plan

We examined the competitor landscape and as a result of this we modernised the logo and then went on to look at the brand imagery and layout.

Design

The large rotating images that we designed really communicate Coffee Seller’s range of products. What’s more, by increasing the size of the images on the product listing pages we were able to increase the chance of sales.

Develop

The website was built in an ecommerce content management system called Magento. We used the existing framework and re-skinned the site with the new design. We also fixed some of the bugs in the system, made the navigation more intuitive and responsive and made the overall site faster to use.
