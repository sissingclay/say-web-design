MDD, a global forensic accounting firm, wanted to update the aesthetic look and feel of the website and make it easier for their clients to understand what sets them apart, how they can help and why employ them.

Plan

The client had several pieces of print collateral that they wanted to combine and translate into an online medium. We started by consolidating the key messages and plotting out how the content could be placed with a simple yet effective layout.

Design

MDD wanted to incorporate design and functionality elements intended to keep the user engaged and to discover deeper content on the site. We designed a new homepage and then rolled this theme out to other key sections of the site.

Develop

The site was developed in WordPress where we were able to add various modules such as the revolution sliders, enabling the required functionality outlined above.
