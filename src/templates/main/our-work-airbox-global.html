www.airboxglobal.com

Airbox Global, a door to door delivery company, came to us with a functioning site but they weren’t happy with the design, layout and some of the way that the site worked. We conducted a complete re-design for them.

← Our work

Plan

We worked closely with the client to discuss what they liked and disliked about their existing website and also viewed their competitors to see what worked and what did not.

Design

We designed a simple yet modern and professional looking website that clearly communicated Air Box’s unique selling points.

Develop

We improved the functionality of the site by making it more intuitive and simpler for users to choose a box and ship their parcels.
